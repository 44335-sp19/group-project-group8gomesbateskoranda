Hello! My name is Rileigh Bates and I am currently studying Interactive Digital Media: New Media at Northwest Missouri State University. I am interested in a variety of subjects, including graphic design, web design, photography, UX and advertising. I’ve wanted to pursue design as a career since high school when I joined yearbook staff as a designer. I enjoy the problem solving that goes into making a good design, along with the creative aspect. As I’ve progressed through college, I’ve learned that I also have a passion for user experience design and advertising. Outside of classes, I like to spend time with friends and family (including my two dogs) and dabble in photography.
